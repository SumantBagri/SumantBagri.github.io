Tool Wear and Remaining Useful Life Prediction in Micro-Milling Along Complex Tool Paths Using Neural Networks

Sumant Bagri, Ashish Manwar, Alwin Varghese, Soham Mujumdar, Suhas S. Joshi

Vol. 71, pp. 679–698, Springer November 1, 2021 DOI: 10.1016/j.jmapro.2021.09.055

Abstract

Conventional tool wear research in micro-milling had been limited to straight tool paths, yet industrial applications frequently demand machining along complex geometries where tool failure risk increases significantly. This paper addresses that gap by developing a data-driven framework — using force, acceleration, and vibration velocity signals captured via a triaxial Kistler dynamometer, accelerometer, and laser vibrometer — to predict tool wear state and remaining useful life under varying tool path radii in micro-end-milling. Tool wear was classified into three regions based on defined criteria, and intermediate optical images of the tool face were captured alongside static radial tool runout measurements. The models — encompassing ANNs and Deep Belief Networks — achieved 93–99% accuracy for RUL prediction, establishing one of the first validated frameworks for TCM under industrially realistic complex-path micro-milling conditions.

Online Tool Condition Monitoring in Micromilling Using LSTM

Ashish Manwar, Alwin Varghese, Sumant Bagri, Suhas S. Joshi

Vol. 36, No. 2, pp. 935–955, Springer December 21, 2023 DOI: 10.1007/s10845-023-02273-3

Abstract

This work proposes a novel approach for high-precision online tool condition monitoring in micro-milling using cutting force signals, correlating dominant frequency variations with tool condition across the complete tool life cycle, and validating the approach across both straight and circular tool paths. LSTM networks were employed to model dominant frequency variations in micro-milling, enabling high-precision online monitoring without interrupting the machining process. The paper extends the prior work on complex tool paths by focusing on real-time, continuous monitoring — a critical requirement for Industry 4.0-compliant smart manufacturing systems targeting micro-mold and die production applications.
